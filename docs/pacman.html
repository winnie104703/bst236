How to play

Step into a Valentine's maze of pockets and passages where Pac‑Man's appetite meets romance. Guide Pac‑Man through the corridors to gobble pellets, avoid (or outwit) the chasing ghosts, and keep an eye out for the rare Rose power‑up — our Valentine's special.

When you collect a rose, the maze becomes charged with romance — but watch the bees. The bees protect the roses and will chase you; collect a rose and Pac‑Man blooms with power, firing hearts in his current direction to fend off the buzzing guardians. Use this Valentine surge to rack up points and clear the maze.
